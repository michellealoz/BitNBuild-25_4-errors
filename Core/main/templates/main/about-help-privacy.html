About Review Radar

How Review Radar Works

Review Radar is a web tool and browser extension designed to help shoppers quickly understand the overall sentiment of product reviews on major e-commerce sites.

  • Paste a Product URL: Enter the product page link from a supported e-commerce website.
  • Automated Review Scraping: Our tool securely extracts customer review texts from the page.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Reviews are processed by a pre-trained AI model to determine if they are positive, negative, or neutral.
  • Keyword & Topic Extraction: We highlight the most discussed product features and issues using advanced keyword and topic modeling.
  • Actionable Dashboard: Instantly view a summary pie chart and lists of top keywords for positive and negative feedback.

Usage Guidelines & Support

  • Supported Sites: Most major e-commerce platforms (e.g., Amazon, Flipkart, Walmart).
  • Use this tool for personal, non-commercial analysis only.
  • If you encounter issues or unsupported URLs, contact support.
  • For best results, ensure your browser extension is updated to the latest version.

Privacy Policy

Data Handling: Review Radar processes product URLs and associated review texts in real-time; we do not store reviews or URLs after analysis is complete.

  • No Personal Data Storage: We do not collect or retain user-identifiable information.
  • Secure Processing: All analysis occurs on secure servers or locally within your browser (for the extension).
  • Compliance: We adhere to basic privacy principles and do not share any data with third parties. For inquiries, email privacy@reviewradar.com.
  • Cookies: Minimal, non-tracking cookies may be used to enhance your experience. You can opt out in your browser settings.
